2023

Freshman

I started my journey in the IT world by learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and since then, I have been striving to develop my skills. I also actively seek out various online communities to expand my network and build connections that can support my career development.

2024

Getting Active And Expand My Capability

Over time, I began to understand more deeply the various roles and divisions that exist in the IT world. Not only limited to the learning process, I also began to share the knowledge I gained with others. I taught and transferred the knowledge I gained, both in technical and conceptual terms, so that others could also develop. In addition, I am increasingly focusing on honing my soft skills, such as communication, collaboration, and leadership, which are very important in the professional world. I have also been actively participating in various communities, both technology-based and broader, such as Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) and several Discord communities. This has given me the opportunity to interact with many people, share ideas, and of course expand my network that can support my career journey ahead. These communities are not only a place to learn, but also a place to collaborate and contribute to the development of a better IT world.

After mastering basic web and app development, I started to develop my skills by creating websites for MSMEs around my neighbourhood. I saw an opportunity to help small businesses utilise technology to expand their market reach. In addition, I also created an online platform for MSMEs so that the benefits can be felt more widely. This project not only improved my technical skills, but also gave me the opportunity to make a positive impact on society and the business world.
